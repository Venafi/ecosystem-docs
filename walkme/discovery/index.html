Discovery¶

Create and manage all of your discovery services. Discovery services do the work of finding your TLS certificates for you. Learn more

  • Create a Basic Discovery service: Quickly discovers certificates inside your private network (no validation).
  • Create an Enhanced Discovery service: Discovers certificates inside your network (includes validation).
  • Use the built-in Internet Discovery service: Discovers certificates outside your company's network.
