Issuing templates¶

Certificate issuing templates are used to configure security policies that are enforced when new certificates are issued.

Issuing templates combine the selection of a CA account with rules that enforce certificate policies, all in a single location. Learn more

Just getting started?¶

Give these a try:

  • Create a new issuing template:

    Click New, give your template a name, fill out the required fields, and then click Save. Learn more

    TIP

    Make it even easier for your certificate requestors to fill out your issuing template: while creating or editing an issuing template, add default settings for them by modifying Recommended Settings.

  • Edit or delete an existing issuing template:

    Click any template from the Template Name column to edit it. Or select one or more templates and click Delete to get rid of them. Learn more
